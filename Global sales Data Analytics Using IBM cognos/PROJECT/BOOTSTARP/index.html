VISUALIZATIONS

We used IBM cognos to create different visualizations for our Global super store Dataset. Here are the list of visualizations:

  • Segment wise Sales, Profit and Qty.Segment wise Sales,Profit and Qty.
  • Use Pie to showcase Sales by Order Priority and Sales by Market.
  • Use a Tree Map to present Sales by Sub-Category
  • Sales and Profit Forecast by Month Country as Region and Region as Filter.
  • Sales vs Profit forecast by Month by Order Priority.
  • Min, Max, Avg Sales by Sub-Category using Box plot.
  • Legacy Map Present Sales vs Profit by Country / Region
  • Present Monthly Sales by Sub-Category using Waterfall chart.
  • Present Sales using Hierarchy Bubbles by Market / Region.
  • Present Sales (Bar), Profit (line) by Sub-Category using Line and Column Chart.

we can also use Tableau to design all the above visualisations.

Our Mission

Our mission is to harness the power of global sales data analytics to drive business growth, optimize operations, and enhance decision-making processes. By leveraging advanced data analytics techniques, we aim to extract valuable insights from diverse sales data sources worldwide, enabling our organization to make data-driven strategic choices, improve customer satisfaction, optimize resource allocation, and ultimately achieve sustainable competitive advantage in the global marketplace."

Our Plan

Define objectives,
Gather data,
Clean and prepare the data,
Design data model,
Import data into Tableau.
Create visualizations,dashboards and story in Tableau.
Analyze and explore the data.
Create reports and presentations in Tableau.
Utilize IBM Cognos for advanced analysis.
Review, iterate, and share ...finally we deploy it.

Our Vision

Our vision for the Global Sales Data Analytics Project is to leverage the powerful capabilities of IBM Cognos and Tableau to gain valuable insights and make data-driven decisions that drive business growth and optimize sales performance on a global scale. By combining the advanced analytics capabilities of IBM Cognos with the intuitive visualizations of Tableau, we aim to create a comprehensive and user-friendly analytics solution that empowers stakeholders across the organization.
